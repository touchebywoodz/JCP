Meeting Minutes & Other Hidden Gems

Meeting Minutes May Mention:

  • Families needing assistance
  • Disciplinary actions
  • Church business involving members
  • Special events and celebrations

Other Records:

  • Sunday School enrollment
  • Choir membership lists
  • Ladies' auxiliary records

Fun Fact: Churches kept records on EVERYTHING!

SPEAKER NOTES (2 minutes)

  • Humor: "Churches were like the Facebook of their time - they recorded everything! Who was 'friends' with who, who got 'unfriended' or disciplined... it's all there."
  • Tip: "When you see disciplinary records, don't just see the negative. These often name spouses, parents, or siblings who came to speak on their behalf, revealing valuable family connections."
  • Explain: "Pay special attention to Ladies' auxiliary records. These were often run by women for women, and they frequently list members by their married AND maiden names, a huge clue for genealogists."
  • Encourage: "It's easy to skip over what looks like boring administrative records, but as you can see, they are filled with hidden gems that paint a rich picture of our ancestors' community lives. Don't overlook them!"
